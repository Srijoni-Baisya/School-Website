Modern English Academy


From excellent teachers teaching the students, numerous school activities held throughout the year and the perfect educational system, Modern English Academy is an English medium co-ed school, following the CISCE curriculum. In addition to the school assessments, students are encouraged to participate in various co-curricular activities, elocutions, quiz competitions, debates, sports events, Independence Day Celebration and many more, which help them to develop interests in several fields. Besides, the school has been the recipient of several awards like the IIHM Award for the Excellence in Academics (ICSE and ISC) Certificate of Merit 2017, British Council International School Assessment Award 2014-2017.
