Modern English Academy


From excellent teachers teaching the students, numerous school activities held throughout the year and the perfect educational system, Modern English Academy is an English medium co-ed school, following the CISCE curriculum. In addition to the school assessments, students are encouraged to participate in various co-curricular activities, elocutions, quiz competitions, debates, sports events, Independence Day Celebration and many more, which help them to develop interests in several fields. Besides, the school has been the recipient of several awards like the IIHM Award for the Excellence in Academics (ICSE and ISC) Certificate of Merit 2017, British Council International School Assessment Award 2014-2017.

Campus Life


School Library

Our school library is well-endowed with a variety of books, including short stories, adventurous ones, detective stories, knowledgeable ones and many more. We often enjoy visiting the library with our friends during a free period and reading our favourite books. Besides, our school librarian issues the different storybooks that we select, and we are allowed to borrrow it for two weeks.

School Canteen

Our school canteen is a favoured place for most of the students, where they can buy wouth-watering dishes and enjoy sharing their tiffin with their friends. Many a times, there is such a rush for a certain food item that we find some of our friends returing from the canteen with an unhappy face, just because the item was finished even before they could reach there, and they have to wait for a while!

Computer Laboratory

We have our computer practical classes weekly in the computer laboratory, and we are always excited to learn new concepts and apply our own ideas. Our teacher guides us, thereby helping us to adapt ourselves with the developing technology. Apart from practising whatever we have learnt in the theory part, we are often divide into groups of two and asked to create a presentation or a project together.



School Activities


English Elocution

The school organizes an English Elocution every year, in which students are eager to participate. Initially, we are asked to memorise certain poems , and our teachers select four to five students among them, who compete for the coveted top three positions.

Art Competition

Students enjoy drawing on various on-the-spot topics using different tools and mediums. With imaginative topics like 'A River Of Dreams', 'A Plate Of Food', 'An Imaginary Animal', students spend a few hours in a creative and relaxed manner. Later, intra-class results are declared.

Yoga (Co-Curricular Activity weekly on Saturday)

A 45-minute yoga class is arranged on every Saturday for students who are a part of this co-curricular activity. Yoga asanas, anatomy, formation, practice, benefits , perfection are performed. Besides, surya namaskar, weight management, core muscle strengthening, flexibilty are practised. Doing pranayama and meditation keeps the mind refreshed, too.

Karate (Co-Curricular Activity weekly on Saturday)

During an energetic karate session, students are taught defensive skills which are very important in modern day life. It helps during the times of life threatening situations. It is systematically taught by a skilled master. From kicks, blocks, kata, students enjoy learning the various kinds of karate exercises.

School Events

Independence Day

Independence Day

Every year, the school celebrates Independence Day with great enthusiasm. After the flag hoisting ceremony, students put up a vibrant programme. The programme features performances like singing, dancing, recitation and many more. Students are given food packets and the day ends on a happy and pleasant note.
Image

Prefects' Investiture

Prefects' Investiture

The Prefects' Investiture ceremony is conducted every session for the senior and junior prefects. On the day of the ceremony for the senior prefects, the school captain and vice-captain, the captains and vice-captains of the four houses are declared. The prefects are given badges after they take an oath. Similarly, on the day of the ceremony for the junior prefects, initially they take their oath and are given their prefect badges, The investiture ends with a closing prayer.
Image

Teachers' Day

Teachers' Day

Students put up a brilliant performance to honour the teachers and their contributions. There are beautiful dance performances, medleys of songs and a drama. Teachers are treated to a scrumptious lunch and the day is worth remembering.
